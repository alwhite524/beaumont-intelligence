Documentation Principles

Intelligence Assessment

An evidence-driven transportation archive

The Potrero Boulevard Interchange represents one of the largest transportation infrastructure investments in Beaumont's modern history.

Because the project spans many years, multiple funding sources, several public agencies, and hundreds of official documents, maintaining a verified source register is essential.

As additional records become available, this Intelligence Center will continue to expand while maintaining the distinction between documented evidence, official actions, and analytical interpretation.
